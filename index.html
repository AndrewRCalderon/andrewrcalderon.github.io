¡Hola!

My name is Andrew Rodriguez Calderón ✌🏼

I am a computational journalist at The Marshall Project, a non-profit newsroom. I work with data & design things for the internet and report on the criminal justice system and immigrants. I also teach data, design and community engagement in the journalism + design department at The New School in New York City.

Previously, I was an investigative data fellow at Columbia University, working on cross-border investigations into U.S.-based, right-wing evangelical groups that use dark money to promote anti-LGBTQ policies in Central and South America.

A few tools I use: Python, R, SQL, HTML5, CSS, Javascript, d3.js, Svelte, Figma, Observable, Twilio, AWS systems, CircleCI, Docker, Terraform, GitHub.
